Favorite Restaurant

Tai Er is my favourite restaurant. They are a Chinese restaurant franchise specializing in food from the Sichuan region. The hallmark of Sichuan cuisine is the spice, and they have the most delicious pickled cabbage and fish dishes.

Favorite Food: Carrot Cake

I love eating carrot cake so much that I have a carrot cake journal where I review all the different carrot cakes I have eaten around the world. After traveling and reviewing the cakes from many different countries, including Dubai, France, Canada, and China, I have concluded that the best way to make carrot cake is with lots of carrots and a fresh cream cheese frosting, nuts optional.
